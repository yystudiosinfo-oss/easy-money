Valuation / Land

A 120mm photographic study on the price of land. This archive uses a grid structure to draw parallel comparisons between the physical landscape and its assigned financial value. Lottery stations were documented across Taiwan — including the offshore islands — as fixed points of informal economy embedded in the landscape.
